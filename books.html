Dreams The Heart Remembers

Genre: Young Adult/Adventure

Type: Novel

Jacob Crawding has acquired a gift that enables him to enter into other's dreams. He has a hard time understanding the purpose of his gift, and the strange dream that seems to be connected to it. After being forced to live with Uncle Rick in Hays, WA, Jacob meets the Jenkins family whose grand-daughter lives with them. After a tragic accident, Jacob goes on a journey to discover what worlds may exist outside of his own reality, in hopes to find a way to save what he loves more than anything else.

ATMOS

Genre: Science Fiction

Type: Trilogy

ATMOS tells the legend of a Somerthian Scout named Orpheus. He and his people seek to inhabit a world known as Atmos, however there are a few set backs. The previous inhabitants of the planet left it in the ruins of nuclear war. This trilogy will cover Orpheus' growth as a Scout and his quest to inhabit the planet of Atmos. Will he succeed? War will surely follow, but with whom?
