About

Hi, my name is Derek! I am a Full-Stack Developer and Professional Creator who currently has his sights set on a web development(mainly frontend) position. Experienced in HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, JQuery, Bootstrap, Firebase, Node Js, MySQL. React, React-Native. I am also effecient in PhotoShop and mostly all Microsoft Office products. I have gained a multitude of problem solving skills from my previous work experience and through my English education that easily translates into web development and team environments. I hope to bring to the world imagination and creativity through my developments and other projects.


I strive to accomplish my goals of being a successful Creator everyday. I am also well-talented in communication, photography, problem solving, and thinking outside the box. I've worked many different jobs, which has helped broaden my skills and talents. I graduated from Utah State University in May 2016 with a Bachelor's degree in English, Creative Writing emphasis. I also have earned a certificate in Web Develpment for the University of Utah. I always strive to work above and beyond expectations and am a quick learner. I adapt well to new situations and work well independently and in a group setting. I believe that hard work reaps benefits for the self and the employer. My others goals are to be a successful author, but I love to share my talents and personality in every job I work. In my spare time, I make videos for my Youtube channel(Creator Kingdom) and work on my many different novels!
